How the demo simulation works

This simulator uses a real public baseline for the 2022 tax gap and then applies a synthetic “AI adoption” model to show how improved triage and detection could change recovered revenue and audit efficiency. The numbers are illustrative—not official forecasts.

Baseline sources: IRS tax gap projection for TY 2022 ($696B gross) and IRS SOI net tax gap projection for TY 2022 ($606B).
