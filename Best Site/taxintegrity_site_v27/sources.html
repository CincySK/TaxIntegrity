IRS — Tax Gap (TY 2022 projections)
Gross tax gap estimate ($696B) and net tax gap projection ($606B), and breakdown components (underreporting).

Supporting documentation for the tax gap estimates and methodology (referenced on-site as “Publication 5869” where applicable).
GAO — AI and tax administration (public explanation)
Explains how AI may help IRS case selection, sampling, and identifying returns more likely to have errors/owe additional tax.
TIGTA — IRS AI governance / inventory (2024 evaluation)
Reports IRS inventory of AI projects and their implementation status.
IRS — Modernization / enforcement strategy updates (2024)
Mentions use of AI/advanced analytics to support selection of complex partnerships for audits and modernization initiatives.
