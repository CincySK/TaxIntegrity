How AI speeds up tax audits (demo)

In real life, audits take time because returns must be selected, reviewed, and validated. Responsible AI can speed up the early steps by prioritizing cases, auto-organizing documents, and surfacing explainable “risk signals” for human reviewers.

✓Triage faster: score & route cases to the right team
✓Document assist: extract key fields and flag missing items
✓Explainable drivers: show why a case is prioritized
Evidence (public sources): The IRS says it is using AI and advanced analytics to help select complex partnerships for audits (IRA Strategic Operating Plan update, Apr 2024). GAO notes AI models are already used for audit sampling and for identifying returns more likely to have errors and owe additional tax (GAO blog, Jun 2024).
Sources: IRS Publication 3744-B (May 2024 update); U.S. GAO blog (Jun 2024); IRS Compliance Presence / Data Book FY 2024.
